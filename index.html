Frequently asked questions

The things people usually want to know before they book.

How many headsets do I need?

As a rough guide: 6 suits a small get-together, 12 a lively party, 18 a big garden do, and 24 a full crowd of around 20 to 25. Guests happily share and swap, so you do not need one each.

How far does the signal reach?

The headsets pick up the transmitter from roughly 50 metres in the open, enough for a typical house, garden or hall. Thick stone walls shorten the range a little.

How does a silent disco actually work?

Music is broadcast wirelessly to everyone's headphones instead of through speakers. Each headset has three channels, so guests can switch between playlists or DJs and take the headphones off to chat whenever they like.

Do you deliver, or do I collect?

We're a local collection and return hire across Berkshire and Oxfordshire. You pick the kit up charged and ready, and drop it back after your event.

What does it cost, and is there a deposit?

Per weekend: 6 headsets are £40, 12 are £60, 18 are £75, and all 24 are £90, each including the transmitters and charging. We also take a refundable £30 deposit at collection, which comes straight back to you when the kit is returned complete and working.

What if a headset gets lost or damaged?

The £30 deposit covers small mishaps and is refunded when everything comes back in good order. While the kit is with you it is your responsibility, and for any loss or damage beyond the deposit we ask you to cover the cost of repair or replacement. It is all set out plainly in our hire terms.

How long do I get the kit for?

The standard hire covers a full weekend. Collect Friday or Saturday and return the next working day. Longer hires can be arranged, just ask.

Are the headphones charged and clean?

Always. Every headset is fully charged, tested and wiped down before collection, so they work straight from the case.
